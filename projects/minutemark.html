MinuteMark

MinuteMark is a precision time tracking and segmentation app built to help users organize time into meaningful intervals. It is designed for people who need a clearer way to measure focus, billable work, and productive sessions without losing simplicity. With structured segments, visual feedback, and intuitive reporting, MinuteMark turns raw time into actionable insight for both personal and professional use.

Productivity SwiftUI iOS

Features

  • Segment-based time tracking
  • Clear visual progress feedback
  • Simple reporting for focused sessions

Tech

  • SwiftUI
  • iOS-first design
  • Local-first productivity workflow
